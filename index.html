My name is James Parkes and I’m a web developer, programmer, and graphic artist from Madison, Alabama. In my free time, I like to play video games, work on independent software projects, and workout at the gym. I’m also a serious collector of video games, beer caps, trading cards, and table top board games. After finishing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from the University of Alabama in Huntsville I took a position as the Digital Web Manager for the Global Marketing Team at Intergraph. In October 2015, Intergraph SG&I rebranded as Hexagon Safety && Infrastructure, so I now serve as the technical lead for the development and maintenance of the company's public-facing website and other associated web projects.

Since joining Hexagon Safety & Infrastructure, I have also taken on other roles within the Global Marketing Team. At the beginning of 2015, I transitioned our team from using Eloqua to using Pardot for marketing automation. During the process, I designed the system by establishing a standard for organizing assets, lead scoring, and branding. After setting up the system, I trained global team members on how to maximize the tool and now serve as the system admin for Pardot. Also, I spearheaded a project to develop a CSS framework for all of our web products. The project is hosted on GitHub and you can find it here.

Finally, I started a Twitch stream back in February 2015. It is a work in progress, but it is something that I've always wanted to do. My channel is mostly a tribute to the games I grew up playing, so a lot of Nintendo 64 and other retro stuff. But, occasionally I play games that are current. If you're interested, check out my channel here.
